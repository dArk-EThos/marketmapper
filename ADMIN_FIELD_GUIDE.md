
# Market Mapper Admin - JSON Field Guide

## Vendor Categories
Select multiple categories that describe what types of vendors this opportunity accepts:

✓ **Food** - Food trucks, restaurants, bakers, farmers with produce
✓ **Craft** - Handmade items, woodworking, jewelry, pottery  
✓ **Art** - Paintings, photography, sculpture, digital art
✓ **Farm** - Fresh produce, plants, flowers, farm products
✓ **Vintage** - Antiques, collectibles, retro items
✓ **Wellness** - Health products, fitness, beauty, aromatherapy
✓ **Other** - Miscellaneous vendors not covered above

## Badges
Choose badges that highlight special aspects of this opportunity:

🔵 **Verified** - Information has been double-checked and confirmed
⭐ **Vendor Favorite** - Popular with vendors, high satisfaction
✨ **New Market** - First-time event, new opportunity  
🔄 **Returning Annual** - Established annual event
🔥 **High Demand** - Limited spots, competitive application
👨‍👩‍👧 **Family Friendly** - Good for family-oriented vendors
♿ **Accessible** - Wheelchair accessible venue and facilities

## Tags
Add descriptive keywords separated by commas. Examples:
- outdoor, seasonal, popular
- indoor, year-round, established  
- farmers-market, weekly, community
- craft-fair, juried, high-end

## Recurring Patterns
Choose the event schedule type:
- **One-time Event** - Happens once (default)
- **Weekly** - Same day every week
- **Bi-weekly** - Every two weeks  
- **Monthly** - Once per month
- **Seasonal** - Summer only, winter markets, etc.
- **Multi-day Consecutive** - 3-day weekend event, etc.
- **Custom Pattern** - Irregular schedule, use description field

## Recurring Description
Provide specific schedule details:
- "Every Saturday 9am-3pm"
- "First weekend of each month, May through October"
- "Tuesdays and Thursdays, summer season only"
- "Last Friday of the month, 6pm-9pm"
