# Market Mapper Research Sources
*Comprehensive list of reliable sources for Manitoba vendor opportunities*

## Official Government Sources
- **Travel Manitoba**: https://www.travelmanitoba.com/events/
  - Official provincial events calendar
  - Festivals and markets listing
  - Regional event information

- **Economic Development Winnipeg**: https://www.economicdevelopmentwinnipeg.com/
  - Business events and trade shows
  - Networking events with vendor components

## Municipal Sources
- **City of Winnipeg Events**: https://winnipeg.ca/
- **City of Brandon**: https://brandon.ca/
- **Rural Municipality websites** - Each RM often lists local fairs/markets

## Festival & Market Aggregators
- **EventBrite Manitoba**: https://www.eventbrite.ca/d/canada--manitoba/events/
  - Search: "vendor", "craft fair", "farmers market", "artisan"
  - Filter by Manitoba locations

- **Facebook Events**: https://www.facebook.com/events/
  - Location: Manitoba
  - Keywords: "craft fair", "farmers market", "vendor opportunities", "artisan market"

## Community & Social Sources  
- **Reddit Communities**:
  - r/Winnipeg - Local event discussions
  - r/Manitoba - Province-wide events
  - r/SmallBusiness - Vendor opportunities posts

- **Third + Bird**: https://thirdandbird.ca/
  - Winnipeg lifestyle and events
  - Market and festival coverage
  - Vendor spotlights

- **Signatures Magazine**: https://signaturesmagazine.ca/
  - Upscale events and markets
  - Art and craft shows
  - Cultural festivals

## Agricultural & Rural Sources
- **Manitoba Agricultural Fairs**: 
  - Individual fair association websites
  - Manitoba Association of Agricultural Societies

- **Manitoba Farmers' Markets Association**:
  - Directory of certified farmers markets
  - Seasonal market schedules

- **Cultural Organizations**:
  - Folklorama pavilions
  - Cultural centers (Ukrainian, Indigenous, etc.)
  - Heritage festivals

## Food & Beverage Events
- **Manitoba Food Processors Association**
- **Local brewery/distillery events**
- **Restaurant weeks with vendor components**

## Seasonal Research Strategy
- **Spring (March-May)**: Summer fair applications open
- **Summer (June-August)**: Fall/winter event planning  
- **Fall (September-November)**: Next year's major events announced
- **Winter (December-February)**: Summer festival vendor calls

## Verification Checklist
For each opportunity found:
1. ✅ Official website exists and loads
2. ✅ 2026 dates confirmed (not recycled 2025 content)
3. ✅ Vendor application process clearly outlined
4. ✅ Contact information is current
5. ✅ Event has occurred previously (not first-time/risky)
6. ✅ Reasonable vendor fees and requirements
7. ✅ Geographic location confirmed in Manitoba

## Red Flags to Avoid
- 🚩 Broken website links
- 🚩 Outdated year references (2025 and earlier)
- 🚩 No clear contact information
- 🚩 Excessive vendor fees without justification
- 🚩 Events that haven't occurred in 2+ years
- 🚩 Social media accounts inactive >6 months
- 🚩 Generic/template content without local details

## Research Workflow
1. **Weekly scan** of primary sources (Travel MB, Facebook Events)
2. **Monthly deep dive** into specialized sources
3. **Quarterly verification** of all existing opportunities
4. **Annual reset** - mark past-year events as historical
