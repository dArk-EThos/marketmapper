About Market Mapper

Market Mapper is a curated directory of vendor opportunities across Canada. We track farmers markets, craft fairs, food festivals, pop-up markets, and more — so you can spend less time searching and more time selling.

Every listing is verified against its source. We don't guess at dates, fees, or deadlines. If we're not sure, we say so.

Built in Manitoba, for Manitoba makers.

How It Works

We Find Them

We scan official sources, organizer pages, and community posts for vendor calls across Canada.

We Verify Them

Every listing is checked against its source. We track dates, fees, deadlines, and application links.

You Apply

Browse, filter, and apply directly through the organizer's link. No middleman.

What We're Not

We're not an application platform. We don't handle payments, jury processes, or booth assignments. We're a directory — we help you find opportunities, and then we link you straight to the organizer. That's it.
